Who We Are

At MiCasa property management services, we believe that every property needs dedicated attention with personalized services to cater all your property management needs, with flexible contracts and meticulous tenant screening with continued team support to give owners and tenants the luxury to lead worry free property maintenance.

  • Our managers are trained to know the properties and their markets resulting in safeguarding and maximizing your investments.
  • Simple, flexible and custom contracts with just a month of notice with no question asked, if you are not satisfied with our services or you cease the need of property manager.
  • Tenant screening, we do meticulous tenant screening with our in-built and standardized process based on our teams industry expertise, helping owners and tenants for smooth and successful lease.
  • Regular routine property checks to make sure your tenant takes good care of the property and there are no maintenance issues.
  • Our focus to give hassle free experience for both parties (Owner/tenant) and pleasant partnership.
